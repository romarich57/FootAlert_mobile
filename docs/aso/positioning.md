# Positioning Strategy - FootAlert

## Store categories

- Apple App Store
  - Primary category: `SPORTS`
  - Secondary category: `none`
- Google Play Store
  - Category: `SPORTS`
  - Tags focus: `live scores`, `football`, `player stats`

## Core message pillars

### EN-US

1. Fast live soccer scores with clear match context.
2. Follow teams and players without creating an account.
3. Useful player and competition stats in a focused mobile UI.

### FR-FR

1. Scores foot en direct rapides et lisibles.
2. Suivi des equipes et joueurs sans creation de compte.
3. Statistiques utiles joueurs/competitions dans une interface claire.

## Positioning boundaries

- Do not claim features that are not implemented in app.
- Keep metadata aligned with actual tabs and flows (`Matches`, `Competitions`, `Follows`, `More`).
- Avoid editorial/news positioning; FootAlert is positioned as a live-score and stats companion.
