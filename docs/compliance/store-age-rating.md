# Store Age Rating And Content Positioning

## Policy

- Product category: football information and alerts.
- Audience positioning: **13+**.
- Prediction content is informational only and must never be presented as betting advice.
- No in-app betting flow is allowed in this product scope.

## App Store Connect (iOS) Checklist

1. Set age rating to at least 13+.
2. Ensure metadata and screenshots do not imply gambling activity.
3. Keep legal links reachable:
- Privacy Policy URL
- Terms of Use URL
4. Include informational wording in store description where predictions are mentioned.

## Google Play Console Checklist

1. Set target age group to include 13+ and exclude children-focused positioning.
2. Complete Content Rating questionnaire consistently with informational sports content.
3. Ensure Data safety form reflects device-centric processing and deletion capability.
4. Keep legal links reachable from listing and in-app settings.

## Release Gate

- Any change to age positioning or prediction wording requires product + legal sign-off before release.
